Let’s talk smart!

Cultural appropiation is very important. One needs to know what to avoid or aim for. There are some things which trigger not the best reaction.
So, don’t burn yourself.

by Lukas Nagy, Oct 22 2021

Hungary is considered a sturdy country where they more-less don’t care about foreign cultural appropriation. For their consistent culture, there is no significant need to make a stand.

But if you though Magyars are not sensitive to their national traits, you are a biiit wrong.

This is how you win their hearts :) ( probably!)

  • Magyars LOVE food

    If you ever go to Hungary or meet Magyars, they love experimenting with food much. Not only that but they do damn love their cusine. It mainly consists of paprika, sour cream, meat, and potatoes.

    If you are vegan by chance, don’t worry! All their delicious recipes can be done in a vegan way too!

    Nonetheless, their love for food overcomes all obstacles

  • Magyars have rich heritage

    From the national to the global aspect, Magyars have much in account.

    This also supports huge nationalism, as they their empire ruled for over hundred of years and was one among the wealthiest in Europe. Their culture is rich customs and their own meaning.

    Half Magyars or non-Hungarian people living outside of Hungary but speaking Hungarian often tend to mark themselves as proud Magyars. Those people feel strong and proud connection to what Hungary achieved.

    1. 482 Olympic medals
    2. Excellent sport teams
    3. Erno Rubik - Rubik’s cube

This is how you win their hearts :) ( probably!)

  • FOOD

    As much as they love food, they are highly sensitive about their cuisine. It comes with complex traditions and you should never critisize the meal and wine that a fellow Magyar prepared.

    Especially, if they come ( food and wine origin) from the same region as your fellow Magyar pal.

  • History

    Hungary has undergone many economic upheveals. They view the history as something darker and may not want to revisit it.

    If you do talk about it, pay attention to your counterpart - Magayars tend to be very sensitive when it comes to losing their wealth in the past.

    They are direct talkers though and don’t mess with anything :)
